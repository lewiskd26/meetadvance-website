How it works

Everything after scoring, connected.

MeetAdvance begins where the timing and meet-management system leaves off.

01

Import Results

Receive official or provisional results through CSV, Excel, approved exports, API integrations, or manual entry.

02

Confirm Qualifiers

Apply configurable advancement rules and automatically identify eligible athletes and relay teams.

03

Accept Advancement

Authorized team administrators accept, decline, or hold each qualifying entry before the deadline.

04

Invoice and Pay

Create one itemized team invoice with every athlete, event, place, fee, adjustment, and payment status.

  • Official, unofficial, protest-period, and award-ready statuses
  • Organization, region, season, and meet-level administration

For teams, clubs, and recreation departments

Know exactly who qualified, what was accepted, and what is owed.

Coaches and team administrators can manage advancement decisions from their phones, share an itemized invoice, and complete one consolidated payment.

  • Accept or decline each qualifying entry
  • Team-level, family-level, or hybrid payments
  • Itemized PDF invoices and receipts
  • Secure links for email, text, or GroupMe
  • Real-time paid, pending, declined, and waived statuses
